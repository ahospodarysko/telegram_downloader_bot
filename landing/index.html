What it does

🎬

YouTube in your quality

Pick 720p or 1080p per video. Formats stay tappable after a download, so you can grab the same link again in another quality.

🎵

MP3 audio extraction

Any YouTube video as a 192 kbps MP3 — music, podcasts, talks — sent back as a playable audio file.

⚡

TikTok, zero clicks

TikTok links download automatically in the best available quality. Share links with trackers are cleaned up first.

📱

Plays right in Telegram

Videos arrive as streaming-ready H.264 MP4, so they play instantly in Telegram's built-in player — no black screens, no saving to disk.

How it works

  1. 1

    Send a link

    Paste any YouTube or TikTok URL into the chat with @toktube_bot.

  2. 2

    Pick a format

    YouTube links show quality buttons — 720p, 1080p, or MP3. TikTok starts immediately.

  3. 3

    Get your file

    The video or audio lands in the chat, ready to watch, forward, or save.

Heads-up: Telegram bots can send files up to 50 MB. For longer videos, pick a lower quality or MP3.

Free & open source

TokTube is a small Python bot built on yt-dlp and python-telegram-bot. Read the code, run your own instance, or open an issue.
